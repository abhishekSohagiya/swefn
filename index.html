About Us

We, at SWEFN Group are united for a mission to help people for their dream of healthy life…!!

  SWEFN Pharmaceutical Private Limited is one of the most trusted pharmaceutical manufacturers in India Having headquarter at Pharma Hub Ahmedabad, Gujrat, India. We have large scale capacity of solid oral dosage form segments – TABLETS & CAPSULES having Capacity for manufacturing 100 million quantity per month.
  Our Plant is located in Pharma Zone at Changodar, Ahmedabad where easily availability of Raw Materials, Packing Materials, Transportation, Man Power and supporting all utility which is favourable for giving Products with high Quality at affordable price.
  We have equipped Best dedicated Automated Production line for Tablets and Capsules which help us to give Qualitative fast production with in time line.
  Our Total Quality Management system adheres to cGMP regulation, and we aim to developing novel and differentiating scientific solutions through innovative products which are design to improve quality of life. We are sure that with our unique business model, corporate values & with culture of quality & excellence we shall lead our relationships with alliances and ultimately create our way to newer heights. With the aim excelling on the path of eminence and novelty, we will continue to dream big, work on its mission and vision, and millions of customers will savour the fruitful outcomes.
  Our pharmaceutical team works diligently by following all the essential guidelines of the government under the strict scrutiny of our management. Apart from these features, our management also takes care of all other relevant and critical issues like obtaining the required certifications from the important organizations and statutory bodies in order to safeguard the interests of the common people. With our fastest growth; today we are one of the leading Indian pharma companies and strive to provide world-class medications at affordable prices.
  SWEFN believes that by working together, we can discover, develop, and deliver the best value to our customers. Our business collaborations are not only based on shared interests, but also on respect, trust and ethical standards.
